The Best Three Point Shooters of All Time in the NBA

By Andrew McBride

We all know that Steph Curry is the best three point shooter, but who else sits at the top with him? I pulled the top 20 three point field goal seasons by individual players, which have all came since the 2012-2013 season, to see how other players stack up with the man who changed the game. And to put it lightly, they simply do not.
